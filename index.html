Stay Safe Online

Cybersecurity Tips

Real-world tips to protect yourself, your family, and your business from online threats.

🔐 WhatsApp Safety

Never Share Your Verification Code — Ever

Hackers steal WhatsApp accounts by tricking people into sharing 6-digit codes. Enable Two-Step Verification now in WhatsApp Settings → Account → Two-Step Verification.

📶 Public Wi-Fi

That Free Airport Wi-Fi Could Be a Trap

Evil Twin attacks clone real hotspots. Always confirm the network name with staff, use a VPN, and never open banking apps on public Wi-Fi.

💳 Online Scams

If It Sounds Too Good to Be True — It Is

Job offers, winning prizes, and urgent money requests are the top scams targeting Kenyans online. Verify before you click, send, or share anything.
