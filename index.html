Stay Safe Online

Cybersecurity Tips

Real-world tips to protect yourself, your family, and your business from online threats in Kenya.

🔐 WhatsApp Safety

Never Share Your Verification Code — Ever

Hackers steal WhatsApp accounts by tricking people into sharing 6-digit codes. Enable Two-Step Verification now in WhatsApp Settings → Account → Two-Step Verification.

📶 Public Wi-Fi

That Free Airport Wi-Fi Could Be a Trap

Evil Twin attacks clone real hotspots. Always confirm the network name with staff, use a VPN or mobile data, and never open banking apps on public Wi-Fi.

💼 Fake Job Scams

Real Employers Never Ask You to Pay for a Job

If any recruiter asks for a registration fee, uniform deposit, or any payment to secure a job — it is a scam. Verify every employer online before responding.
