Cybersecurity Tips

Real-world tips to protect yourself, your family, and your business from online threats.

🔐 WhatsApp Safety

Never Share Your Verification Code — Ever

Hackers steal WhatsApp accounts by tricking you into sharing a 6-digit code. Enable Two-Step Verification now in WhatsApp Settings to protect your account.

Learn more →
📶 Public Wi-Fi

That Free Airport Wi-Fi Could Be a Trap

Evil Twin attacks clone real hotspots. Always confirm the network name with staff, and use a VPN or your mobile data instead when possible.

Learn more →
💳 Online Scams

If It Sounds Too Good to Be True — It Is

Job offers, prize winnings, and urgent money requests are the top scams targeting Kenyans online. Always verify before you click or send anything.
